üêà Cats Are Not Small Dogs

Cats aren't just "low-maintenance dogs." They are a completely different species with their own body language, medical needs, and stress signs. Treating them like small dogs often means missing early warning signs when something is wrong.

  • They hide pain instead of crying or limping.
  • They're obligate carnivores with different nutrition needs.
  • Stress shows up as hiding, not barking or whining.
  • "Independent" doesn't mean they don't need care and attention.

Meow and Furrever exists to remind people that cats deserve thoughtful, cat-specific care.

About This Project

Meow and Furrever was created for an Information Systems application development course to combine coding practice with a real-world topic: responsible cat care.

Many people think of cats as low-maintenance or ‚Äúindependent,‚Äù which often leads to skipped vet visits and missed signs of illness or stress. This project focuses on correcting those assumptions by giving owners clear, cat-specific information about health, behavior, and daily care.

The long-term goal is to turn this into a simple, user-friendly app that can store multiple cat profiles, rotate through myths and tips, and encourage more proactive, informed care for indoor cats.
